Grammar-Translation Method

The Grammar-Translation Method is a traditional language teaching approach that emerged in the 19th century and was widely used in foreign language education until the mid-20th century. This method primarily focuses on teaching the written form of a language and emphasizes the rules of grammar and translation between the target language and the native language.
